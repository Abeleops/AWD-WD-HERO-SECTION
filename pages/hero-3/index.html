Introduction

"Black Leg" Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. Born as the third son of the Vinsmoke Family, he's known for his incredible kicking techniques, chivalrous attitude, and world-class culinary skills.

With a bounty of 330,000,000 berries, Sanji is one of the crew's top fighters, using his powerful legs to deliver devastating kicks while keeping his hands safe for cooking.

Backstory

Born into the royal Vinsmoke Family, Sanji was ridiculed by his father and brothers for his compassion and love of cooking. After escaping his abusive family, he was shipwrecked and stranded on a rock with the pirate chef Zeff.

For 85 days, they survived with limited food, with Zeff sacrificing his own leg to save young Sanji. After rescue, they founded the Baratie restaurant, where Sanji honed his cooking skills until joining Luffy's crew.
