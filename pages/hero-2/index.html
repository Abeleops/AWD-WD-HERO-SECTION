Roronoa Zoro
Roronoa Zoro is the sword-wielding powerhouse of the Straw Hat Pirates, aiming to become the world’s greatest swordsman. With his iconic Three-Sword Style, he’s fearless, loyal, and always ready to cut down any challenge.
Roronoa Zoro started as a lone bounty hunter with a promise to become the world’s greatest swordsman. After joining Luffy’s crew, he faced powerful foes and pushed his limits in every battle. Loyal and fearless, Zoro’s journey is one of discipline, sacrifice, and unbreakable will.
Zoro’s greatest enemy is Dracule Mihawk, the world’s strongest swordsman. As the one who holds the title Zoro seeks, Mihawk represents both his ultimate rival and the goal of his lifelong dream. Their first clash ended in Zoro’s defeat, but it fueled his resolve to surpass Mihawk one day.
