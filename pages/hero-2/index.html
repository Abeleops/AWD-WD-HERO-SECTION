Roronoa Zoro

Wado Ichimonji

Roronoa Zoro's most treasured sword, representing his promise to his childhood friend Kuina. This white blade is one of the 21 Great Grade Swords.

As his most iconic weapon, Zoro places this sword in his mouth when using his signature Three-Sword Style technique.

Sandai Kitetsu

A cursed sword that Zoro obtained in Loguetown. Despite the shop owner's warning that it brings death to its wielders, Zoro tested his luck against the blade's curse and won.

The third generation of the famous Kitetsu swords, this blade has a bloodthirsty nature that Zoro has tamed through his incredible willpower.

Enma

The only sword known to have wounded Kaido, once wielded by the legendary samurai Kozuki Oden. This powerful blade forcibly draws out its user's Haki beyond their control.

Zoro received this Great Grade Sword in Wano as a replacement for Shusui, dramatically increasing his cutting power while presenting a new challenge to master.
